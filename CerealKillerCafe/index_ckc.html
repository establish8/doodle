The world’s first international cereal cafe opened in 2014 by cereal obsessed twin brothers, Alan & Gary keery. The 90s themed nostalgic cafes are situated in Brick Lane and Camden in London, or you can visit us in Dubai mall. At cereal killer we don’t just eat our cereal from bowls, we use it in all our menu items, from chicken to ice cream, and alcohol to milkshakes. We are a dining experience that won’t be forgotten, not matter your age you’ll leave or cafes feeling like a kid again.
